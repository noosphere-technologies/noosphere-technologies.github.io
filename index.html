Trust Agility: Putting Trust Back in Human Hands

API-first trust services that enable the agile authenticity layer of the Internet.

Centralized moderation is often indistinguishable from censorship. It's time to empower everyone to make their own decisions about sources and networks they trust.

Intuitive and Human-Centered

Built for humans. Provides fine-grained, content-specific trust indicators right at the point of consumption, when people are trying to make sense of digital media.

Easy to Use

Makes trust relationships transparent and manageable for non-technical people, even after application trust stores have been bootstrapped.

Distributed and Federated

No single entity makes moderation or censorship decisions. Everyone owns their content and are insulated from arbitrary censorship and de-platforming.

Modern, Sleek, Sophisticated

An API-first approach, CLI, SDKs,and toolkits make it easy for application developers to tap trust signals generated and aggregated by the Noosphere platform.

Modernizing the Web's Trust Layer

User experience is the key to trust

Organizations across industries are struggling to combat synthetic media and disinformation. Algorithms, platform moderation and even some journalistic practices have contributed to the spread of untrustworthy information.

Shifting media consumption habits are eroding trust. As people move away from traditional media sources, synthetic media, propaganda and deepfakes exploit the lack of trust infrastructure and protocols. Similarly, trust relationships that do exist are constrained to the ‘walled gardens’ of social media platforms.

Noosphere makes trust relationships transparent and manageable, even after application trust stores have been bootstrapped.

We’re here to build technology and services that let individuals, not platforms or governments, decide what they trust, and what they don't.
— Andrew Brown, Co-founder

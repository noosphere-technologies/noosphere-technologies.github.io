Who Can You Trust?

Crisis of Trust on the Internet

Malicious actors are using disinformation to influence human behavior. This intentional attempt to erode trust in journalism is undermining civil society.

Synthetic media, propaganda, and deepfakes exploit the lack of trust infrastructure and protocols. Disinformation poses convincingly alongside legitimate news.

Algorithms and platform moderation contribute to the spread of untrustworthy information. Algorithms promote content that draws engagement, incentivizing creators to be ever more extreme.

Centralized moderation is often indistinguishable from censorship. When platforms moderate extreme content, the authors cry foul.

The foundation for trustworthy media

A scalable, distributed trust solution for digital media

Noosphere enables trustworthy machine-to-machine and machine-to-human transactions by integrating real-time identity, integrity, and validation checks with the individual user’s trust preferences.

Secure: State-of-the-art distributed cryptography improves Web Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) to safeguard against malicious actors.

Modern: Cloud-native approach and approachable tooling insulates developers from the low-level trust mechanics of legacy tech.

Flexible and extensible: API-first approach enables app, platform and infra developers easily integrate and innovate on trust services. Platforms can leverage Noosphere to implement trust-based guardrails and expose trust context.

The power is in your hands

Noosphere is agile, modern, and transparent

Noosphere allows consumers to make informed choices about what they trust without needing to dive into the nuts and bolts to assess its trustworthiness (unless they choose to). Trust decisions are portable across platform, accessible to any application that taps Noosphere APIs.

Real-time: Trust cues are reliably and securely exposed to a reader at the point of consumption, at the moment they are assessing the trustworthiness of an artifact.

Professionally curated: Producers use simple language to express relationships, which propagate automatically across the network. Trust relationships are continuously reconfigured as networks evolve.

Agile and Flexible: Readers can actively configure and modify their store of trust, which filters how curated trust relationships are exposed within applications.

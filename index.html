The power is in your hands

Noosphere is agile, modern, and transparent

Noosphere allows consumers to make informed, persistent choices about sources they trust. Trust decisions are portable across platform, accessible to any application that taps Noosphere APIs.

Real-time: Trust cues are reliably and securely exposed to a reader at the point of consumption.

Professionally curated: Producers and independents use simple language to express relationships, which propagate automatically across the network.

Agile and Flexible: No more dark pattern algos. Readers can easily configure and continuously curate their store of trust anchors and filters.

Our mission is to provide meaningful, secure trust indicators that are helpful to everybody, across newsfeeds, messaging, social, search, advertising, and gaming.
— Jonathan Brown, Co-founder

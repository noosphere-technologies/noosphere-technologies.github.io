Protecting Human Trust in the AI Age

Organizations across industries are struggling to combat synthetic media and disinformation. Algorithms, platform moderation and even some journalistic practices have contributed to the spread of untrustworthy information.

Shifting media consumption habits are eroding trust. As people move away from traditional media sources, synthetic media, propaganda and deepfakes exploit the lack of trust infrastructure and protocols. Similarly, trust relationships that do exist are constrained to the ‘walled gardens’ of social media platforms.

Trust Agility: Putting Trust Back in Human Hands
